Description

The Digital Games Research center is a multi-disciplinary center whose focus investigates the scientific, engineering, artistic, social and educational challenges of digital entertainment. Join in this work to examine the future of computer games, including innovative applications of games technologies to bear on problems relevant to social, medical, training and educational contexts.

This summer:
  • Create dashboards for data analytics on educational simulations and competitive video games.
  • Help researchers develop and prototype novel physical interfaces to interact with educational simulations.

Responsibilities

  • Beta test and provide feedback on virtual laboratory simulations.
  • Develop visualization and analytics dashboards for gameplay data.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Preferred Experience & Participant Qualities

  • Is comfortable learning and using programming languages
  • Can adapt to change and flexible assignments
  • Comfortable working in teams and collaborating
  • Ability to work independently without constant oversight (shows self-direction, initiative)
  • Completed at least one computing course or equivalent camp/outreach program

Skills Required

Self-starter, ability to work in a team, problem-solving skills, excellent verbal communication skills, creativity, attention to detail, patience, time management.

Benefits to the Student

By participating, you can get intern credit hours for your school, interact with NC State faculty and students, and contribute to the future of computer gaming. Meet and collaborate with other high school students interested in computing. Additionally, our program staff would be more than willing to write you letters of recommendation for any scholarship, college, or other program applications.

How To Apply

You will need a google account to fill out our application. Link to google form: https://go.ncsu.edu/cs-intern-application

Personal Statement

Your 1-page personal statement should include answers to the following questions and can also include any additional information that will help you be a standout candidate.

  1. Why are you interested in this position?
  2. How will this benefit your educational or career goals?
  3. Do you watch or play competitive sports or video games? If yes, describe your most memorable experience while playing or watching.
